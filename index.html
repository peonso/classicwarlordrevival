Classic Warlord Revival intends to be a reset to bring back to life good memories from cards through the whole Warlord CCG lifespan. A fresh new start to be figured out, full of flavor, giving high emphasis on sub class traits. It's meant to allow a healthy metagame with many viable strategies and an approach that allow the skill playing the game to have higher impact than the decklist you bring to play (as opposed to Ancients). It is still a work in progress, on playtest phase. Left navigation bar allow you to check the visual spoiler, you can also check card list spreadsheet here.

As in the Ancients format, due to the older wording on many cards involving the targeting rules, the following rule is in place:

An action "targets" a card when:
1) The action declares the card as a target.
2) At the time the action is performed, it directly modifies the attributes, location or readiness of a card in play or in the discard pile, or requires it make a check, test, or save.
